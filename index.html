Indian Centre for Social Science and Management Research

The Indian Centre for Social Science and Management Research (ICSSMR) is a premier institution dedicated to advancing research, education, and knowledge dissemination in the fields of Management Studies and Social Sciences. Registered under the Micro, Small & Medium Enterprises (MSME), Government of India, ICSSMR serves as a dynamic platform for academicians, researchers, professionals, and students to engage in meaningful academic and professional pursuits.
